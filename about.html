About This Website

Home

This website serves as a curated, ultra-lightweight gateway to essential free and open-source software tools that help schools and communities reduce dependence on Big Tech.

Our goal is to promote digital autonomy by showcasing tools that respect privacy, encourage sustainability, and foster inclusivity — all while being fast, minimal, and accessible.

Images and media are optional and load only when requested by users, to save bandwidth and energy. The site is designed to work even in simple terminal browsers and on slow internet connections.

Contribute

We welcome contributions! If you know of other free and open-source tools that fit our mission or have ideas to improve this website, please visit our GitHub repository and open an issue or pull request.
