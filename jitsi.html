Jitsi Meet is an open-source video conferencing platform that requires no account and offers secure communication.


Visit the official website: jitsi.org/jitsi-meet

Key Features

  • No account needed to join meetings
  • End-to-end encryption support
  • Screen sharing and chat
  • Self-hosting options
